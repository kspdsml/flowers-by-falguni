Hi, I'm Falguni
"We don't just create bouquets we create emotions."

I'm a home-based floral artist based near Garden City, NY. What started as a passion for bringing beauty to celebrations has grown into a small business built entirely on word of mouth, love, and fresh flowers.

Every arrangement I create is made to order, by hand, with the care and attention you'd expect from a friend not a factory. Whether it's a traditional Indian wedding garland, a heartfelt Mother's Day arrangement, or something completely one-of-a-kind, I work closely with you to bring your vision to life.

I specialize in South Asian ceremonies and celebrations varmala garlands, Navratri décor, Diwali arrangements, and puja thali flowers as well as prom season, memorial wreaths, and all the everyday moments that deserve something beautiful.
