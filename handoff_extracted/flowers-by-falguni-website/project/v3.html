The Story

Made with
love from home

"We don't just create bouquets — we create emotions."

Hi, I'm Falguni — a home-based floral artist based near Garden City, NY. What started as a passion for bringing beauty to celebrations has grown into a small business built entirely on word of mouth, love, and fresh flowers.

Every arrangement I create is made to order, by hand, with the care and attention you'd expect from a friend — not a factory. Whether it's a traditional Indian wedding garland, a heartfelt Mother's Day bouquet, or something completely one-of-a-kind, I work with you to bring your vision to life.
